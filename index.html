AABHAAR Scheme

A Scheme for Felicitation of Superannuated Employees · Corporate HR Circular No. 650/2025 · Effective 01-04-2025

The AABHAAR Scheme is framed by POWERGRID to felicitate and reward superannuated employees in recognition of achieving age milestones — acknowledging their loyalty, invaluable contributions, and lasting legacy. It covers monetary rewards for age milestones (65 to 100 years), felicitation on the Annual Raising Day with family, personalised doorstep delivery for senior/aged beneficiaries, and a one-time past-milestone clause for employees who crossed a milestone before the scheme launched.

The attached document summarises all three AABHAAR circulars issued so far (launch & framework, implementation guidelines, and the 2026 protocol amendment) in one place, followed by the full text of each circular.

Preface

Over the years, POWERGRID Corporate HR Department has issued a number of circulars, office memoranda and scheme documents governing medical attendance, treatment and reimbursement facilities available to serving as well as retired employees under the Post-Retirement Medical Benefit (PRMB) Scheme and also the AABHAAR Scheme to felicitate and reward superannuated employees in recognition of achieving age milestones. These circulars, being issued at different points in time and on different aspects of the rules, are often scattered and difficult to locate when actually needed.

This Compendium has been prepared with the specific objective of consolidating all such circulars — classified and grouped based on nature/type of the circulars in date wise descending order sequence with a ready-reference Index — so that a Retired Employee, spouse or dependent can quickly locate the applicable provision, rate, ceiling or procedure without having to search through multiple individual documents.

This compilation is based on the circulars, office memoranda and scheme documents hosted on the Retired Employees Portal (https://tejas.powergrid.in/ex-employee/) as on 30-06-2026.

Usefulness of this Compendium to Retired Employees

  • Single-window reference: All circulars relating to Preventive Health Check-up, Hearing Aid reimbursement, Artificial Appliances/Prosthetics rates, Cataract (IOL) lens ceiling, Ayurveda treatment, Dental rates, Day-care procedures, and PRMB dependent coverage are available at one place.
  • Quick navigation: The Index gives the circular number, date, subject and exact page range in the full Compendium file, enabling a retired employee to go directly to the relevant circular instead of searching multiple files.
  • Up-to-date position: Since circulars are arranged in descending date order, the employee can immediately see the latest applicable provision on any subject, and also trace how a particular rule has evolved over time.
  • Assistance in claims: Approved rates (dental, prosthetics, hearing aid, cataract lens, Ayurveda therapies, Holy Family Hospital Delhi Rates, Batra Hospital Delhi Rates, Dr Lalpath Labs rates) and eligibility conditions are readily available for use while preparing and submitting reimbursement claims under the PRMB Scheme.
  • Awareness of procedural requirements: Circulars relating to PRMB Life Certificate submission, updation of dependents on the Power-Setu portal, and the AABHAAR felicitation scheme help retired employees stay compliant with annual submission requirements and avail all eligible benefits without interruption.

It is hoped that this Compendium will serve as a handy and reliable reference document for the Retired Employees' community of POWERGRID, and will be updated periodically as and when new circulars affecting medical reimbursement are issued.
